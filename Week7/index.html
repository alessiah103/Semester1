Welcome to VIAPets

Located in the heart of Northern Europe, VIAPets is more than just a pet shop, it is a place where every animal is treated as a Very Important Animal (VIA). Founded by Bob Oldenuff, a retired football manager, VIAPets began as a traditional pet store, selling a variety of pets. As pet ownership surged, we expanded our services to include a kennel, where pets can be cared for when their owners are away. At VIAPets, we pride ourselves on offering high-quality care for all pets, whether they’re looking for a new home or simply a place to stay while their owners travel. With a unique “recycled pet” approach, we provide a second chance for animals who need new families, giving each a new beginning. Our kennel services ensure pets are kept comfortable and safe, making us a trusted choice for all pet lovers in the community. Visit VIAPets to meet our incredible animals, learn more about our kennel services, and see why every pet we care for is truly special!

History

The journey of VIAPets began with a simple dream to bring joy and companionship to both pets and their owners. Initially a small pet shop nestled in the heart of Northern Europe, VIAPets quickly became a beloved part of the community. As more people discovered the joy of pet ownership, the demand for reliable and compassionate pet care grew, inspiring VIAPets to expand its services. Today, VIAPets is not only a pet shop but also a trusted kennel where animals find a safe and caring environment when their owners are away. Guided by the unique concept of “recycled pets,” VIAPets offers second chances to animals in need of new homes, connecting them with families ready to offer love and care. Over time, VIAPets has evolved into a community-focused establishment, hosting adoption events and providing personalized care programs for every animal. From creating a warm, welcoming space to partnering with local shelters, VIAPets is dedicated to the happiness and well-being of both pets and owners. Now a cherished institution, VIAPets continues to uphold its mission of treating every animal as a Very Important Animal, making it the go-to destination for pet lovers across the region.
