Kennel Services: A Safe and Cozy Stay for Your Pets

At VIAPets, we understand that leaving your pets in someone else's care can be a big decision. That’s why our kennel services are designed to provide a safe, comfortable, and engaging environment for pets of all kinds while you’re away. Our dedicated staff ensures each pet receives personalized care, attention, and love—just like they would at home. With spacious kennels, daily exercise routines, and playtime sessions, your pets will enjoy their stay, whether it’s for a day or an extended period.

We also prioritize health and hygiene, ensuring all pets in our care have clean, comfortable accommodations. Plus, our experienced team monitors pets closely to keep them happy and safe. Trust us to give your pets a cozy home away from home!

Adoption & Rehoming: Giving Pets a Second Chance

We believe every pet deserves a loving home. Through our adoption and rehoming service, we offer a welcoming place for pets that are no longer able to stay with their original families. After thorough health and behavior assessments, we prepare these pets for their next chapter by finding them the right match with loving new owners. Our rehoming approach helps reduce pet abandonment and offers a "recycling" approach to pet care, giving each animal a second chance at happiness. Whether you’re looking to adopt or need assistance rehoming a pet, we’re here to help pets find the right family for a fresh start.
