VIA

VIA University College is one of the largest universities of applied sciences in Denmark with 8 campuses in 7 cities all across the Central Region of Denmark.

International atmosphere

VIA offers more than 40 study programmes in English – from bachelor’s and master’s degrees, AP degrees and top-ups to exchange programmes, summer schools and other short term programmes – in a wide array of academic disciplines including health sciences, engineering, design, business, education, film and animation and much more. We have around 18,500 students of which more than 2,500 are internationals.

Life at VIA

Independence in learning, personal responsibility and freedom characterize the student experience at VIA. All our study programmes consist of a combination of theoretical teaching and practice and most have obligatory internships at real workplaces. Outside of school there are plenty of options to engage in extracurricular activities both on and off campus.

Entrepreneurial spirit

VIA has been awarded with the prize as Denmark’s leading entrepreneurial institution of higher education in 2014, 2018 and 2019 and entrepreneurship in an integrated part of all study programmes. In addition, VIA offers guidance and counselling to students starting their own businesses.

Sustainability

Another key theme at VIA is sustainability. All VIA students have circular thinking, sustainability and the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals on their curriculums, which means that VIA graduates will be able to contribute to a sustainable agenda and development in their future workplaces.
